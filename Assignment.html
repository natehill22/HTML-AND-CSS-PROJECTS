Pudding

It can be a bit confusing, but some puddings are not sweet at all! For example, "steak and kidney pudding" is a savory (not sweet) dish. It's similar to "steak and kidney pie" but has a softer pastry. Another famous savory pudding is Haggis from Scotland.
