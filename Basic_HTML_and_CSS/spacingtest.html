Bee Facts

 -Bees have two large compound eyes and three smaller simple eyes on the top of their heads, which help them detect light changes and navigate.

 -Honey bees communicate the location of food sources using a special dance called the "waggle dance".

 -In its lifetime, a worker bee produces only about 1/12th of a teaspoon of honey.

 -The hexagonal shape of honeycomb is the most efficient design for storing honey with no empty space.
